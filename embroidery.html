Embroidery

Resources to dive in

If this is your first time embroidery the following steps describe the process to begin crafting:

  1. Gather your materials. If you plan to stitch freehand you only need thread and needle, if you plan for a more structured approach, get a loop and a pattern.
  2. Find a good source of light: it could be a window or a lamp.
  3. Undetermined articles in Spanish are “un, unos, una, unas.” The undetermined articles in English are “a, an”.
  4. Thread your needle with at least two strings of thread. The number of strings change the feeling of the design, and you can cover more surface faster when you have more strings. I usually use 3 or 4 strings.
